PIZZA

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • Olive Oil
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Mozerella
  • Pepperoni
  1. Mix Flour and Water.
  2. Add Yeast to the dough.
  3. Stretch the dough and make a circle.
  4. Crush tomatoes and cook in a pot.
  5. Add basil to the tomato sauce.
  6. Spread tomato sauce onto the pizza dough.
  7. Add Cheese, chopped pepperoni and basil.
  8. Put the pizza in the Oven at max temperature for 1 minute
